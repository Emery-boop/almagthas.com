Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What makes AL-MAGTHAS different from other real estate firms?

AL-MAGTHAS combines premium service with innovative solutions. Our focus on client relationships, attention to detail, and comprehensive approach to property management and development sets us apart. We prioritize long-term value creation over short-term gains and maintain the highest ethical standards in all our operations.

How do you determine property values for consultancy services?

Our valuation process combines market analysis, comparable property assessments, income potential evaluation, and physical property inspection. We use industry-standard methodologies and proprietary tools to provide accurate, defensible valuations that reflect true market conditions.

What geographic areas do you serve for estate management?

We currently manage properties throughout Abuja and surrounding regions. Our services are expanding to cover additional major metropolitan areas. Contact us to discuss management for properties outside our current service areas - we may still be able to assist or recommend qualified partners.

Do you offer customized service packages?

Absolutely. We understand that every property and client has unique needs. Our service packages can be tailored to include only the services you require, with flexible terms and pricing structures. We'll work with you to create a solution that fits your specific objectives and budget.
