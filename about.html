Comprehensive Approach

ACS shall continue to adventure in many returns derivable from being property professionals in the constructed and developed location. We established on the improvement of property and rent or sale to improved or developed property (buildings) for purchaser or tenants.

Innovation & Execution

We coordinate all of these activities, converting ideas from paper to real property. We take the greatest risk in the creation or renovation of real estate and receive the greatest rewards.

End-to-End Services

Typically, we tract land, determine the marketing of the property, develop the building program and design, obtain the necessary public approval and financing, and rent out, manage, and ultimately sell it.

OUR RESPONSIBILITIES TO OUR SERVICES

  • As professionals in property management, we are obligated to understand the Tenants or buyer needs, respond to their requests, process procedures of purchase, collect rents in a timely manner, and monitor their activities as regards the purchase or lease clauses requirements.
  • ACS shall determine appropriate and balanced purchase and rental rates agreeable to the environment of the property.
  • We shall remain resolute in recommending and implementing positive marketing programs, special promotions and advertising strategies to the Owner in order to maximize occupancy and rental rates.
  • We shall handle effectively for the Owner, tax, profit and loss, budget and financial statement issues and analysis of the real estate property.
